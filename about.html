About Catalyst Hiring Solutions

Who We Are

Catalyst Hiring Solutions is India's trusted recruitment consultancy, specializing in hiring consultancy, BPO recruitment agency services, manpower consultancy and remote hiring solutions. Founded in 2015, we've placed over 50,000 candidates across India in companies ranging from startups to Fortune 500 enterprises.

Our Mission

To connect India's best talent with the right opportunities. We believe in data-driven recruitment, transparent processes, and long-term partnerships with both candidates and employers.

What We Do

We provide end-to-end hiring consultancy across multiple sectors:

  • BPO Recruitment: Voice and non-voice staffing for contact centres and business process outsourcing firms
  • Manpower Consultancy: Contract, permanent and dedicated team staffing solutions
  • Remote Hiring: Work-from-home job placements and remote-ready candidate screening
  • Bulk Hiring: Large-scale recruitment programs with structured sourcing and assessment

Why Choose Us?

Local Expertise

We have deep knowledge of India's recruitment market across all major cities — Bangalore, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Chennai, Pune, Delhi and more.

Data-Driven Approach

We use psychometric assessments, skill evaluations and AI-driven shortlisting to match the right candidates for your roles.

Fast & Reliable

Average time-to-hire: 7-14 days. We maintain quality standards while being quick to deliver results.

Compliance-Ready

All candidates are background-checked, documentation verified and tax-compliant. We handle employment contracts and statutory requirements.
